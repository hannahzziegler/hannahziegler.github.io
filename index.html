I'm Hannah Ziegler


I'm an ambitious, young reporter studying journalism and information science at the University of Maryland.

I currently oversee a staff of more than 100 student journalists as the editor in chief of The Diamondback, my university's independent student newspaper. In this role, I edit stories for structure, clarity, accuracy and style five nights per week. I also organize coverage and work to solve newsroom-wide issues by implementing long-standing solutions.

I am also currently reporting on business and investment news as an intern at Barron's, one of the country's most trusted financial publications. So far, I've focused my coverage on commodities, economics and consumer issues.

My reporting and data work has been featured in Barron's, the Baltimore Sun, Maryland Matters, The Atlanta Journal-Constitution, Montgomery Community Media and many other news outlets across Maryland.

I am a reporter who writes and codes, using programs like R and Python to collect, analyze and parse data for short and long-term stories. I hope to combine my passion for data journalism and traditional reporting in any future beat I have.

When I'm not reporting or coding, I enjoy running, painting and spending time with my family back home in Michigan.
